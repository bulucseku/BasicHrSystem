For whom is this tool intended?

BusinessIQ is intended for anyone who needs to perform analyses or use data to do their job. BIQ is a more free-form tool that allows users to explore data, generally without restrictions. For those who want to drill into the data, generate insights, share findings with others, test hypotheses, and generally understand the data, BIQ is the tool for you. Many other different types of users typically need to consume reports to make tactical decisions or identify areas in need of attention. Dashboards, because the visualizations are predefined and the drill-paths set ahead of time, are ideal for users who do not want to explore or create reports from scratch.
