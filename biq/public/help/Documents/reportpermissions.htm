Report Permissions

The available actions you can perform on a report depend on whether or not you were the original author of that report. For reports that you created, you can perform all possible actions (See Details, View, Edit, Copy, Delete, Share). On the other hand, you cannot edit a report that has been shared with you by another user. Depending on your configuration, you may or may not be able to re-share a report that has been shared with you.

If you would like to either edit or share a report that has been shared with you, the easiest thing to do is to create a copy of the report and give it a unique name. You will then have full permissions to edit and share the report copy.
