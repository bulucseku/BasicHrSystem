Edit a Booklet

There are several ways that a Booklet can be edited:

11. Editing which reports are included in the Booklet.

12. Editing the order in which reports appear in the Booklet.

13. Editing the contents of an individual report in the Booklet.

The sub-sections included here explain how to perform each of these actions. All of them begin with finding the Booklet that you want to open and clicking EDIT to open in edit-mode.
