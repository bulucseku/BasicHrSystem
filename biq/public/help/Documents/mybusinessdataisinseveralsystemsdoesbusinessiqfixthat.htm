My business’ data is in several systems. Does BusinessIQ fix that?

Depending on your exact configuration, the answer is a qualified “yes”. An intuitive user interface isn’t worth much if the data is disjointed or otherwise hard to interpret. Before MarketMover is deployed, Sentrana works with your company to determine which data it wants to include in the system, and then works to cleanse that data. You should have a separate document prepared by Sentrana that explains how your company’s data is integrated in BIQ and the Dashboards, what the column names mean, and how the different fields can be combined.
