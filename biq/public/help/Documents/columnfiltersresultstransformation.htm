Column Filters: Results Transformation  

After a report has been executed, you are able to take advantage of column filters using the Results Transformation dialog. Column filters allow you to sip through the report grid, limiting the rows you want to see in a column based on a specific selection. For example, if you only want to see the shipped cases for a specific Product Group in your product hierarchy, all you have to do is click on the Results Transformation icon, expand the column header, select that particular product group and click OK. You can select more than one filter option.

BIQ will then automatically update the report chart and report grid based on your selection.

It is important to keep in mind that any column filter selections made in either the Builder page or the Reports and Booklets page do not persist; they are simply visual changes that cannot be saved to the report you are viewing and are discarded when you perform any other action or navigate away from the page.
