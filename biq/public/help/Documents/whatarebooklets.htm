What are Booklets?

Booklets allow you to stitch together multiple saved reports into a multi-page presentation style layout. Booklets are intended to behave like document-style presentations but with the added ability to interact with the charts and filter the data as you page through the individual reports.

Booklets are not affected by any changes that you make to a saved report, even if that report has been “added” to a Booklet. This is because when you create a Booklet from saved reports in your catalog, you are actually creating copies of the selected reports that are now specific to that Booklet.
