Standard Filters

Filters are arranged in the user interface in the panel on the right-hand side and are grouped by dimension. You will notice that most if not all of the dimensions available in the column panel also appear in the filter panel. When you click on a dimension in the filter panel, the section will expand to reveal the selection options. Depending on how many elements you can choose from, you may see different screen controls, such as check-box lists, collapsible trees, and other views. You may sometimes, depending on the selection mechanism, need to hold the CTRL key while you click on the available options to select more than one filter.

A standard filter (which we will usually refer to simply as a ‘filter’) will be applied to every metric in the report definition.
