Viewing a Saved Booklet

To view a Booklet in presentation mode as opposed to edit-mode, click VIEW when you want to open the booklet instead of edit. The Booklet Composition panel will not be visible, and instead you will have just the report to view on the screen (you can also minimize the Saved Reports panel to maximize your viewing space).

Navigation through the booklet works the exact same way as in preview mode. Note that, for individual reports that are part of the booklet you are viewing, you can still show/hide the grid or chart, filter columns and metric ranges, and interact with the charts and tables as usual. Changes made, including range based and column based filters, will not be saved to individual reports in your booklet.
