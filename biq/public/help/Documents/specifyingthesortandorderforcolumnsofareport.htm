Specifying the Sort and Order for Columns of a Report

If you want the primary sort to be on a specific column, click on the Results Transformation icon (explained in detail in a later section) at the bottom left corner of the Report Definition panel. When the Results Transformation dialog appears, click on a column header to expand it. The order value for that column can be adjusted by clicking the up and down Order arrows. To indicate that you want your primary sort to be on this column, you should change to order value to “1”.

The radio buttons in the dropdown column menu determine whether the sort on this column should be ascending or descending. To confirm your selections, click OK. In addition to using the Results Transformation dialog, you can also sort your column(s) by clicking anywhere in the column header itself.
